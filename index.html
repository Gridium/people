Health benefits

Gridium offers health, dental, and vision insurance to all full-time employees.

Our health insurance is through Blue Cross Blue Shield California, which is available to anyone in the United States, regardless of geography.

We offer two PPO plans, a Gold and a Platinum. Platinum offers more generous benefits but comes with higher premiums. You'll need to compare the plans yourself to decide which makes more sense for you, but in general Platinum is for people who know that they will consume a lot of health care, in which case the more generous benefits offset the higher premiums.

Some additional details:

  • Gridium covers 100% of the cost of the premium for the Gold plan. Employees who choose the Platinum plan must cover the difference in cost between the two plans.
  • Gridium covers 50% of the premium for dependents. Dependents include children, spouses, and domestic partners.
  • Gridium offers an additional $1,500 benefit to defray the cost of your deductible. More on that below.
